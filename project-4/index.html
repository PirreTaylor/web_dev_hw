What is Responsive Web Design?

What are you visiting this website on? Your cell phone? Your home computer? Is the content on this page clear one your device screen?

Responsive Web Design is the practice of styling a website so that the content within will properly scale to and arrange in a way that fits the screen that you are viewing it on. The more responsive your website's design is, the wider the pool of users that can access and use it.
