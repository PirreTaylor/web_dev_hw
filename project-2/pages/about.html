About the Author

Whether it be for an early-mornng class or an all-night session of slaving away at an impending final's week project, caffeine is a necessity in any college student's life. As a student currently striving towards obtaining her online Bachelor of Fine Arts degree, webpage author Taylor Pirre finds herself frequently in the latter situation. With coffee being a substance too heavy in the caffeine department for the lightweight author to handle, she turned towards its distante cousin, hot tea, in hopes of finding a stable, less panic-enducing alternative to the beverage. The world of tea proved to be one

Today, the author continues to find new ways to share her passion for hot leaf juice by introducing others to the wide variety of flavors found in the many unique forms of tea.
